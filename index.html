Methodology¶

To test the performance of all (at the time) known QSO reconstruction pipelines at predicting the Lyman-alpha (Lyα) line profile (1180-1260Å) from a small, unified sample of QSOs. For each QSO, the QSO flux was blinded (removed) prior to performing the QSO flux predictions. In total, 60 QSOs were reconstructed, with 30 QSOs each obtained from X-Shooter (XQ-100) and SDSS (BOSS DR16).

Reconstruction pipelines¶

In total 9 different reconstruction pipelines were utilised for this comparison project. Below, a brief summary of each is provided along with links to the original publication.

  • Greig: Covariance matrix reconstruction based on the measured line-correlation strengths between Lyα, N V , C IV, Si IV + O IV doublet and C III obtained from a large training set of low-z QSOs. Line profiles are fit with a mixture of single and double component Gaussian profiles characterising the line width, height and velocity offset.
  • Davies: Spectral decomposition of the QSO flux following principal component analysis (PCA). Predict the blue-side (<1280Å) PCA components from the red-side components by applying a projection matrix trained on a low-z sample of QSOs.
  • QSANNdRA (Ďurovčíková): An extension of the Davies approach, connecting the blue-red side PCA components with an artifical neural network (ANN). Consider a larger and new training set of QSOs along with more PCA components.
  • Fathivavsari: Neural network reconstruction of the QSO flux within each spectral bin between 1170-1290Å based on the observed QSO flux around C IV, the Si IV + O IV doublet and C III.
  • Spectre (Reiman): An extension of QSANNdRA utilising normalising flows to provide a probabilistic distribution of reconstructed QSO profiles.
  • iQNet (Liu): Simple auto encoder network to predict the Lyα flux based on redward information.
  • Bosman: An extension of the Davies PCA based reconstruction approach with a larger training set.
  • QFA (Sun): unsupervised and probabilisitic reconstruction approach based on latent factor analysis.
  • Meyer: An information maximising variational auto encoder is used to predict the Lyα profile based on the redward information.
